About

Kakashi Hatake (Japanese: はたけ カカシ, Hepburn: Hatake Kakashi) is a fictional character and one of the main protagonists in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi.
In the story, Kakashi is the teacher of Team 7, consisting of the series' primary characters, Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno.
Kakashi's past has been extensively explored in the series, resulting in a gaiden being devoted to his past experiences.
Kakashi has appeared in several pieces of Naruto media, the featured films in the series, the original video animations, and the video games.
